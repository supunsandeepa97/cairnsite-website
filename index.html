How it works

Five steps, in order — like stones on a trail.

Each stage builds on the last. You never pay attention to a thing until there's something real in front of you.

  1. 01

    We find you

    We look for a local business that does great work but has no website — and decide you're worth building for.

  2. 02

    We build a real sample

    Not a mockup. A genuine, working site for your business — pages, copy, and layout — built fast by humans and AI.

  3. 03

    We show you

    First a few screenshots, so you can see it at a glance. Then a live link you can click through and try yourself.

    Screenshots first, then a working link.

  4. 04

    You decide

    No pressure either way. If it isn't right for you, that's fine. If you love it, we move to the last step.

  5. 05

    We host & maintain it

    We put it live on your own domain, keep it running, and look after updates — so you can get back to your business.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does Cairnsite work?

We design a real, working sample of your website first. You see it live on your phone — and only if you love it do we publish it on your own web address and host it for you.

Do I pay before I see my website?

No. We build the sample first, so you can see exactly what your site looks like before you decide anything.

Can you build a website for any business?

Yes — cafés, salons, restaurants, tradespeople, shops and more, anywhere in the world. Every site is custom-built from your real details.

How do I get started?

Just message us on WhatsApp. Tell us your business name and we'll prepare a free sample website for you to look at.
